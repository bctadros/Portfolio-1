About Me




Welcome to my home page,

I was born on December 28th 1992 in Caracas, Venezuela. After graduating High School I decided to move to Miami so I can follow my dream as an Artist.
I enrolled in Miami International University of Art and Design to study Visual Arts. I'm in my senior year of college and will soon be graduating in June 2015.

I love working with many mediums but my favorite one is color pencils.

I hope you enjoy my work!
